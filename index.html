Why this retreat is different

Most health advice starts in the wrong place.

"Fix your gut." "Take this supplement." "Cut carbs." None of it works long-term because it skips the upstream. Your body breaks in a specific order:

Stress→ Hormones→ Sleep→ Gut→ Metabolism→ Inflammation→ Everything else

Fix the top of the chain, and the downstream repairs itself. That's the Blueprint. This retreat teaches you the full system — not isolated hacks — so you understand what to do, why it works, and in what order.
